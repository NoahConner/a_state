Company History and Establishment Year

Founded in 2018, A-State Insurance began with a simple but impactful mission: to make insurance clear, accessible, and reliable for all Texans. From the start, we partnered with multiple carriers to provide personalized coverage for homes, vehicles, businesses, health, life, and surety bonds. Over the years, we’ve grown steadily while remaining fully committed to exceptional service, multilingual support, and an understanding of the unique needs of every individual and community we proudly serve across Texas.

Core Mission & Values

At A-State Insurance, our mission is to provide Texans with insurance solutions built on trust, reliability, and clarity. We value strong relationships, personalized guidance, and serving every community with respect and care. By combining professional expertise with a commitment to honesty and support, we ensure clients feel confident, protected, and understood. Our focus is on helping families, businesses, and individuals secure the coverage they need while keeping the process simple and dependable.

Types of Clients Served

A-State Insurance proudly serves a wide range of clients, including families, automobile owners, homeowners, and businesses of all sizes. We focus on building trust and providing reliable coverage tailored to each client’s unique needs. With a commitment to community and personalized service, our multilingual team ensures everyone feels supported, understood, and confident. From protecting homes and vehicles to safeguarding businesses and lives, we help Texans secure what matters most with care and integrity.
